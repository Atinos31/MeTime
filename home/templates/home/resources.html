For People With Mental Health Problems

If you have, or believe you may have, mental health problem, it can be helpful to talk about these issues with others.
It can be scary to reach out for help, but it is often the first step to helping you heal, grow, and recover.
Having a good support system and engaging with trustworthy people are key elements to successfully talking about your own mental health.
